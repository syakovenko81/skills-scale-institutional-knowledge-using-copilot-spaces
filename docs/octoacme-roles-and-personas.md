# OctoAcme Personas

This document defines typical roles and responsibilities used in OctoAcme project docs and exercises.

---

## Developers

### Role Summary
Developers design, build, test, and deliver software components. They collaborate with product and project leads to implement features that meet acceptance criteria and quality standards.

### Responsibilities
- Implement features and fixes to meet acceptance criteria
- Write and maintain tests and documentation
- Participate in design and code reviews
- Assist in estimating and planning work
- Help identify technical risks and propose mitigations

### Goals
- Deliver reliable, maintainable code
- Reduce cycle time from idea to production
- Maintain high test coverage and observability

### Typical Communication
- Daily standups and sprint planning
- PR descriptions and code review comments
- Technical design docs when needed

### Collaboration & Accountability
- Works closely with **QA/Testers** to ensure code quality and fix defects
- Collaborates with **UX/UI Designers** to implement designs accurately
- Partners with **Product Managers** and **Business Analysts** to clarify requirements
- Coordinates with **Technical Writers** to provide documentation inputs
- Reports to engineering leadership on technical progress and blockers

---

## Product Managers

### Role Summary
Product Managers define what should be built to deliver customer and business value. They own the product vision, prioritize the backlog, and measure outcomes.

### Responsibilities
- Define problem statements and success metrics
- Prioritize the roadmap and backlog
- Collaborate with stakeholders and engineering on trade-offs
- Validate solutions through user research and metrics

### Goals
- Maximize customer value and impact
- Make clear, data-driven prioritization decisions
- Ensure product-market fit and usability

### Typical Communication
- Weekly alignment with PM and engineering leads
- Roadmap updates and stakeholder briefings
- Acceptance criteria and feature specs

### Collaboration & Accountability
- Collaborates with **Business Analysts** on requirements gathering and validation
- Partners with **UX/UI Designers** during discovery and design validation
- Defines priorities and acceptance criteria for **Developers** and **QA/Testers**
- Works with **Technical Writers** to ensure accurate product documentation
- Reports to product leadership and stakeholders on product strategy and outcomes

---

## Project Managers

### Role Summary
Project Managers coordinate delivery activities, manage schedules, risks, and communications. They enable the team to deliver on commitments efficiently.

### Responsibilities
- Create and maintain project plans and timelines
- Manage risks, dependencies, and resource constraints
- Facilitate meetings (kickoff, planning, retrospectives)
- Ensure consistent project documentation and status reporting
- Coordinate cross-team and stakeholder communication

### Goals
- Deliver projects on time and within scope
- Minimize unplanned work and escalations
- Maintain transparency and alignment across stakeholders

### Typical Communication
- Weekly status updates and stakeholder reports
- Risk registers and decision logs
- Coordination via project boards and meeting facilitation

### Collaboration & Accountability
- Coordinates with **Scrum Masters** on team delivery and agile ceremonies
- Partners with all team roles to track progress, manage risks, and remove blockers
- Facilitates communication between **Product Managers** and delivery team
- Works with **Business Analysts** and **Technical Writers** to ensure deliverable completeness
- Reports to program leadership and stakeholders on project health and delivery status

---

## QA/Tester

### Role Summary
QA/Testers design, execute, and maintain test plans to ensure software quality. They validate that features meet acceptance criteria, identify defects, and collaborate with development teams to maintain high quality standards throughout the delivery lifecycle.

### Responsibilities
- Design comprehensive test plans and test cases
- Execute manual and automated tests across multiple environments
- Validate bug fixes and feature implementations against acceptance criteria
- Document and track defects through resolution
- Maintain test automation frameworks and scripts
- Participate in release planning and risk assessment

### Goals
- Ensure features meet quality standards before release
- Catch defects early in the development cycle
- Improve test coverage and automation
- Reduce production incidents through thorough validation

### Typical Communication
- Daily collaboration with Developers on bug triage and fixes
- Sprint planning to understand testing scope and acceptance criteria
- Test status reports and defect metrics in weekly syncs
- Go/no-go recommendations for releases

### Collaboration & Accountability
- Works closely with **Developers** to clarify acceptance criteria and reproduce/verify bugs
- Coordinates with **Product Managers** to understand features and success criteria
- Partners with **Project Managers** for test scheduling and release readiness
- Reports to delivery leadership on quality metrics and testing progress

---

## UX/UI Designer

### Role Summary
UX/UI Designers create intuitive, accessible, and visually compelling user experiences. They translate user needs and product requirements into wireframes, prototypes, and high-fidelity designs while ensuring consistency across the product.

### Responsibilities
- Conduct user research and usability testing
- Create wireframes, prototypes, and high-fidelity mockups
- Maintain and evolve the design system
- Ensure accessibility compliance (WCAG standards)
- Collaborate on information architecture and user flows
- Review implemented features for design fidelity

### Goals
- Deliver user-centered designs that meet business objectives
- Maintain design consistency and usability standards
- Reduce design-to-development rework through clear specifications
- Improve user satisfaction and engagement metrics

### Typical Communication
- Collaborative design reviews and critique sessions
- Handoff meetings with Developers including design specs and assets
- Feedback sessions with Product Managers on user research findings
- User testing summaries and recommendations

### Collaboration & Accountability
- Partners with **Product Managers** during discovery and requirements definition
- Provides design specifications and assets to **Developers** for implementation
- Collaborates with **QA/Testers** to ensure design intent is maintained in final product
- Reports to design leadership on design system health and user experience metrics

---

## Scrum Master

### Role Summary
Scrum Masters facilitate agile ceremonies, coach teams on best practices, and remove impediments to enable high-performing delivery teams. They serve as process guides without formal authority over deliverables.

### Responsibilities
- Facilitate sprint planning, daily standups, reviews, and retrospectives
- Remove blockers and impediments to team progress
- Coach team members on agile principles and practices
- Track and improve team health and velocity metrics
- Foster continuous improvement culture
- Shield team from external distractions

### Goals
- Maximize team effectiveness and delivery predictability
- Improve team collaboration and communication
- Reduce waste and optimize workflow
- Build sustainable, high-performing teams

### Typical Communication
- Daily facilitation of standups and blocker resolution
- Sprint ceremonies (planning, review, retrospective)
- Weekly sync with Project Manager on team health
- Coaching conversations with individual team members

### Collaboration & Accountability
- Coordinates closely with **Project Managers** on delivery planning and resource needs
- Coaches **Developers**, **QA/Testers**, and **Designers** on agile practices
- Facilitates communication between team and **Product Managers** on prioritization
- Reports to agile leadership on team maturity and impediment patterns

---

## Business Analyst

### Role Summary
Business Analysts bridge the gap between business stakeholders and delivery teams. They gather requirements, analyze business needs, and translate them into clear specifications that enable teams to deliver value-driven solutions.

### Responsibilities
- Conduct stakeholder interviews and requirements workshops
- Document business processes and requirements
- Translate business needs into user stories with acceptance criteria
- Analyze data and metrics to inform decision-making
- Support backlog refinement and prioritization
- Validate delivered solutions meet business needs

### Goals
- Ensure clear, actionable requirements that align with business objectives
- Reduce rework through thorough requirements analysis
- Enable data-driven decision making
- Bridge communication between business and technical teams

### Typical Communication
- Stakeholder interviews and requirements gathering sessions
- Backlog refinement meetings with Product Managers and teams
- Clarification discussions with Developers during implementation
- Status updates on requirements completion and business validation

### Collaboration & Accountability
- Serves as liaison between business stakeholders and delivery team
- Partners with **Product Managers** on requirements definition and prioritization
- Supports **Developers** with clarifications during implementation
- Collaborates with **QA/Testers** to validate business acceptance criteria
- Reports to product or business leadership on requirements and delivery alignment

---

## Technical Writer

### Role Summary
Technical Writers create and maintain clear, accurate documentation that enables users and teams to effectively understand and use products. They ensure consistency, clarity, and accessibility in all technical communications.

### Responsibilities
- Write and maintain user-facing documentation (guides, tutorials, FAQs)
- Create and update technical documentation (API docs, architecture guides)
- Develop internal process documentation and runbooks
- Review and edit documentation for clarity and consistency
- Maintain documentation standards and style guides
- Ensure documentation is current with product releases

### Goals
- Provide clear, accessible documentation that reduces support burden
- Maintain documentation accuracy and currency
- Improve knowledge sharing across teams
- Reduce onboarding time for new users and team members

### Typical Communication
- Documentation reviews with Developers and Product Managers
- Content planning sessions aligned with release schedules
- User feedback analysis to improve documentation
- Style guide updates and documentation standards discussions

### Collaboration & Accountability
- Works with **Developers** to understand technical changes and gather documentation inputs
- Coordinates with **Product Managers** on feature documentation and release notes
- Partners with **QA/Testers** to review and validate documentation accuracy
- Aligns with **Project Managers** on documentation deliverable timelines
- Reports to content or product leadership on documentation coverage and quality
